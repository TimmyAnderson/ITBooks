ENTITY FRAMEWORK CORE vsak umoznuje vykonavat QUERIES, ktore referuju na PRINCIPAL ENTITIES a DEPENDENT ENTITIES a vracia PROPERTIES tychto ENTITIES BEZ TOHO, aby sa pouzil LINQ OPERATOR [Include].
